About TEDxChennai

We, at TEDxChennai, believe that the bedrock of a successful sustainable ecosystem is an ensemble of symbiotic relationships that work towards re-imagining the wheel rather than re-inventing it.

With this underlying principle, we have embarked on a quest to provide a platform where ideas are not only shared but are born, by bringing the community together, challenging perspectives and powering conversations that truly matter.
